How it started

We Saw a Problem and Decided to Fix It

When it comes to buying a mattress or bedframe in Canada, most people face the same frustrating experience — Facebook Marketplace listings with high prices and questionable quality, or big box stores with long delivery waits and complicated financing options.

We asked ourselves a simple question: why should a Canadian family have to wait days for a mattress, pay inflated prices, or settle for low-quality products? The answer was — they shouldn't.

In 2024, MattBed Canada was born in Calgary with one goal: to give every Canadian family access to high-quality mattresses and bedframes at fair prices, delivered to their door the same day they order — with zero advance payment required.

We started with Calgary and have since expanded to Edmonton, Vancouver and Red Deer — with more cities planned as we grow.

Our mission

Quality Products. Honest Prices. Same Day.

At MattBed Canada we believe that everyone deserves to sleep well — regardless of their budget, credit history or how recently they arrived in Canada.

"Our mission is simple — give every customer more value than they expect. Better products, lower prices, faster delivery and a service experience that makes them tell their friends."

Every mattress and bedframe we deliver is carefully selected for quality. Every price is set to give our customers genuine value — not inflated margins. And every delivery is made the same day because we know that when you need a mattress, you need it now.
