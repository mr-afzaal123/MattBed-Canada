About Mattbed Canada

Making quality sleep accessible — delivered to your door today

Who we are

Mattbed Canada is an affordable same-day mattress and bedframe delivery service operating across Edmonton, Calgary, Vancouver, and Red Deer. We started with one simple belief: getting a good mattress shouldn't mean waiting 2–3 weeks, paying online before you've seen the product, or renting a truck to pick it up yourself.

What we sell

We stock a full range of mattresses — high-density foam in 5", 7", 9", 10" and 12" thickness; memory foam for pressure relief; orthopedic and hybrid mattresses for firm back support; and pillow top mattresses for a plush, hotel-style sleep. All available in single (38×75"), double (54×75"), queen (60×80") and king (76×80") sizes.

We also deliver bedframes — platform frames and metal frames — in all sizes. Order a queen mattress and bedframe together and we deliver both in the same trip at no extra delivery charge.

Who we serve

Our customers include newcomers and immigrants from South Asia — India, Pakistan, Bangladesh — who need a mattress immediately after arriving in Canada without a credit card. We serve students moving into their first apartment, families furnishing new homes, and anyone who simply needs a comfortable mattress delivered today, not next week.

We understand what it means to arrive in a new city and need to set up a home fast. That's why we've made our ordering process as simple as possible — just fill in a form or send us a WhatsApp message, and we handle the rest.

How we work

Order via our website form or WhatsApp before 4pm and we deliver the same day. You pay cash on delivery — no credit card, no online payment, no deposit. We carry your mattress or bedframe to your room, you inspect it, and you pay only when you're satisfied it has arrived.

Our cities

We currently serve Edmonton, Calgary, Vancouver and Red Deer with free same-day delivery on most items. We're expanding — more cities coming soon.
