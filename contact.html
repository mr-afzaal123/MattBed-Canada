Frequently asked questions

Do you deliver the same day?

Yes — order before 4pm and we deliver the same day in Edmonton, Calgary, Vancouver and Red Deer.

Do I need a credit card to order?

No. We accept cash on delivery only. You pay when your mattress or bedframe arrives at your door — no upfront payment required.

What sizes do you carry?

Single (38×75"), Double (54×75"), Queen (60×80") and King (76×80"). All available for same-day delivery.

What types of mattresses do you sell?

Foam, memory foam, orthopedic, hybrid and pillow top — in 5", 7", 9", 10" and 12" thickness.

Is delivery really free?

Yes — free delivery on most items across all 4 cities. No hidden fees.

Can I order in Urdu or Punjabi?

Yes! Just message us on WhatsApp and we'll help you in your preferred language.
